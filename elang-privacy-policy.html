Privacy Policy for eLang Language Training App

Effective Date:September 27, 2024

At eLang, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how it is used, and the measures we take to protect it. By using the eLang app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you sign up for eLang, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and language preferences. We collect only the minimum amount of information necessary to provide you with our services.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data about how you use the app, including interactions, progress, and preferences, to improve your learning experience.
  • Device Information: We collect device information, such as your device’s operating system, to ensure the app functions correctly.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our language training services.
  • Personalize your learning experience and adapt content to your progress.
  • Send important updates or notifications regarding the app, including account-related emails.
  • Ensure the app functions properly on your device.

3. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as you have an active account. If you decide to delete your account, your data will be permanently deleted from our system inmediately uppon request.

4. Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your data and account, you can initiate the process through the app. After requesting account deletion, you will receive an email with a confirmation link to finalize the process. Once confirmed, all your data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share data with third-party service providers to help us operate the app, such as cloud storage providers. These providers are required to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

6. Children’s Privacy

The eLang app is intended for users aged 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

7. Security of Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, you can contact us at appgisinc@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app, and you are encouraged to review the policy periodically. Your continued use of eLang after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the changes.
