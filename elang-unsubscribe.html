How to Delete Your Data and Account for eLang Language Training App

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from eLang, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the eLang app and go to the settings menu.

  2. Select the option labeled "Delete Account" or "Unsubscribe".

  3. Once you confirm, we will send an email to your registered email address with a link to complete the deletion process.

Click on the link provided in the email to permanently delete your account and data from our system. Please note that this action is irreversible, and you will lose access to your account and any stored progress.

If you encounter any issues or have further questions, feel free to contact our support team.
